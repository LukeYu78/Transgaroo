About us

Melbourne is World renowned for its cycling community. However, the cycle crashes have been on the rise in the past few years especially in the City Of Melbourne. There are reports that suggest a considerable amount of cycling enthusiasts refusing to take up cycling citing the increase in the accidents. Reports also suggest that an inexperienced Male within the age of 18-29 is 4 times more likely to be involved in an accident. Recently, there have been acccident fatalities from international riders and it was ascertained that most of the inexperienced cyclists are not fully aware of the cycling rules and that the cyclist is much safer on the cycling lanes than on normal roads. Furthermore, Melbourne is sufferning from increasing road congestion. The City of Melboure suggests encouraging more people to take up Cycling as one of the biggest measures to combat congestion on the roads. The City has a vision 2030 to reduce the vehicular congestion by 4 times. We at Transgaroo, want to provide a comprehensive solution which provides a fit to the awareness problem. MoveMelb is a one stop platform for the inexperienced cyclists to plan his trip in the city by finding

  • All Cycling paths from a location in the City of Melboure
  • The Nearest Cycle Parking Locations on these paths
  • The nearest public Drinking Fountains on these paths
  • And Finally and most importantly, provides all the locations for cycle crash hotspots on these paths to apprise the cyclist of potentially accident prone areas

    • Overall, We at Transgaroo, envision to foster a safer cycling environment for the inexperienced international cyclists riding in the City Of Melboure by providing all the aforementioned artifacts. Consequentially, we believe, a safer cycling environment will enourage more cyclists to take up cycling as a means of commute in the City which would supplement the Council's mission 2030.
