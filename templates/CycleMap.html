Path Finder

The PATH FINDER feature of MoveMelb is the One stop platform to get the all the safety indicators and additional features.

  • Whether you want to explore the City or you want to go to a particular location, MoveMelb has you covered. We provide "Wandering" & "Destination" services.
  • Once you have selected your location, click on "Find All Paths". This will show you the cycling paths from the location of your choice. The paths are shown by safety indicators as well.
  • You can avail our location services by checking any of the check boxes to see the locations of Toilets, Drinking Fountains, Crash Hotspots, and Cycle Parking Locations
  • In case, you dont find a dedicated path, you can draw your own route by using by clicking on the icons on the upper right hand corner of the Map
  • After you have found the path of your desired path, you can take a snapshot of the map by clicking the Download button.
